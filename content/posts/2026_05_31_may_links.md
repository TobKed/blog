## Some thoughts

### [Half Quill](https://halfquill.com/)

My collegue created the [Half Quill](https://halfquill.com/) which is collaborative writing platform. Its core concept is "picking up where stranger left off". If you want to exercise your brain, practice writing skills and prevent your brain from rotting that may be the way. Also no pressure to write, you can just read and see where some other people minds drifted away. I even see that [latest update](https://halfquill.com/updates/b1c46059-04ae-47d7-9175-d387b78a6928) allows you to download stories as ebooks. This is getting better and better.
