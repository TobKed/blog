I am aggregating here some more or less interesting stuff of various IT related materials which I came across this month.

> A [recent paper](https://www.pnas.org/doi/abs/10.1073/pnas.2221311120) I encountered suggests that the rate of learning among students doesn’t actually differ all that much.
